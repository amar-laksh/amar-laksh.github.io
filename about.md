<p>
	Hello, I am the Codeless Text.
</p>
<p>
I despise every bit of these magically contorted strings of pure reason and creative intellect that people so lovingly pronounce as "Code".
This "Code" which - so they say - beautifully elaborates and collaborates with the musings of those self-appointed "scribes" called programmers.
Oh to think of it! People calling themselves programmers!
</p>

<p>
They think that Code sets them free! it makes things happen! Oh what a shame they don't know how dangerous this vile creature is!
They do not know about the tentacles of this never-ending and supposedly "self-documenting" creature, which slowly and surely reaches down into the programmer's system with its helpful "warnings" and mystical syntax errors.
I say this Code is evil.
</p>

<p>
Evil because how do you explain the 3 a.m. insomnia that the petty programmer suffers daily?
How do you explain 10 different compilers with their 10 different warnings just to mess up the programmer so they change whitespaces?
How do you explain tabs VS. spaces? (both are just blank space for Gods' sake!)
How do you explain vi VS. emacs? (author's note: not even touching that.)
How do you explain Linux VS. Linux? (I heard this Linux thing won)
</p>

<p>
The harsh, sad and true fact is... You cannot.
</p>

<p>
These sick programmers love it! They want to make it more and more bigger and some say even more terse at the same time!
Now, I tell you, Let's get rid of this Code! I say enough with C files! enough with .py and .sh scripts!
I say let's bring back the .txts!
</p>
<p>
Let's make text Great Again! Make it, The Codeless Text!!
</p>
